Projects

Here are my currently active projects, in no particular order. When no collaborators are listed, it can be assumed that Stephanie has her hand in the project.

  • System FC with kind equality. We propose an update to FC, the internal language of GHC, that brings kind equalities to the language. The new System FC merges types and kinds and has the (*:*) axiom. The draft paper (and extended version), submitted to ICFP 2013, is online. I have implemented these ideas into GHC. With Stephanie Weirich and Justin Hsu.
  • Overlapping type family instances in Haskell. What changes are necessary to the surface language and in GHC to allow overlapping type family instances? See the GHC wiki page for more info. This feature is now part of GHC and will be included in the next major release. With Simon Peyton Jones and Stephanie Weirich.

Below are my less active projects. Most weeks do not involve any work on these, but issues crop up now and again.

  • singletons. This project supports the singletons package, allowing a limited form of dependently typed programming in current versions of Haskell. The paper resulting from this work was published at the Haskell Symposium, 2012.
  • System FC in GHC. I wrote up a formal specification of the internal language within GHC here.
  • Ironclad C++. We define a subset of C++ that can be proved not to allow access to invalid memory locations. A draft paper has been submitted to OOPSLA 2013. With Christian DeLozier, Peter-Michael Osera, Santosh Nagarakatte, Milo M. K. Martin, and Steve Zdancewic.
